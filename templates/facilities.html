Our ENT Facilities

Nasal Allergies & Nose Surgeries

We take up all types of allergy testings and allergy specific treatments. Our team is well versed with performing surgeries for deviated nasal septum, sinus issues, fracture nasal bone & nasal augmentation.

Endoscopic sinus surgeries

We have an elaborately advanced set up for performing endoscopic sinus surgeries and various other surgeries where advanced scopes are required. Our team is highly trained, exceptionally skilled and routinely does various endoscopic procedures.

Ear Infections & Surgeries

We treat all kinds of bacterial, fungal, viral & non specific ear infections. Our patients come with complaints of ear discharge, pain, itching among various other problems. We frequently perform surgeries for perforated ear membrane, middle ear problems and mastoid infections.

Hearing problems and Hearing aids.

We do hearing tests clinically in the opd as well as pure tone audiometry in a proper sound proof room with qualified personnel. We try to find out root cause of hearing problem and treat accordingly. We also provide optimum Hearing aids manufactured by class leading Indian and foreign companies.

Throat infections and surgeries

We routinely treat a number of patients having tonsillar infections, pharyngeal and laryngeal infections, adenoid issues on OPD basis and perform surgical procedures as required according to the treatment.

Thyroid problems

We conduct all thyroid function tests, ultrasonography and other radio imaging tests along with thyroid related clinical examination. We prudently decide the most appropriate treatment, whether its surgical or medical.

Head & Neck Cancers

We work along with oncosurgeons to come out with the best possible approach in diagnosing and managing various Head & Neck cancers including oral cavity carcinomas, laryngeal cancers, thyroid tumours & Nasopharyngeal carcinomas.
