Dr. Tanush Shah

An ENT Specialist you can trust.

Dr. Tanush Shah has gathered expertise to treat all problems related to Ear, Nose and Throat. His patients come to him for a varied range of treatment like ear infections, nasal allergies, hearing problems, sinus issues, throat infections and many other ENT related problems.

Dr. Shah is an attentive listener who tries to understand the root cause of all ENT conditions, asks to the point questions and gives the right medications accordingly.
